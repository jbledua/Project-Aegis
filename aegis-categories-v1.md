# Project-Aegis  
## Audit Categories & Subcategories (v1.0)

This document defines the canonical audit categories and subcategories used by Project-Aegis.
Each subcategory is intended to be scored independently using a standardized rubric.

---

## Operations
Governance, planning, validation, and organizational control maturity.

### Governance & Process
- Documentation & Knowledge Base  
- Change Management  
- Incident & Problem Management  
- Monitoring & Alerting  

### Resilience & Continuity
- Recovery Planning (RTO / RPO)  
- Disaster Recovery Procedures & Testing  

### Lifecycle & Vendor Governance
- Patch & Update Governance  
- Asset Lifecycle Management  
- Vendor & SaaS Governance  

### Auditing & Assurance
- Control Auditing & Evidence Collection  
- Periodic Access & Configuration Reviews  
- Policy Compliance & Attestation  
- Third-Party & Security Review Readiness  

---

## Users
Identity, access, and user-centric risk management.

### Identity & Access Management
- User Account Lifecycle (Joiner / Mover / Leaver)  
- Authentication Controls (MFA, Conditional Access)  
- Privileged Access Management  
- Access Reviews & Least Privilege  

### Credential & SaaS Protection
- Password Management (Password Manager Adoption)  
- Microsoft 365 / Google Workspace Backup  
- User Data Retention & Recovery  

### Human Risk & Enablement
- Security Awareness Training  
- Phishing & Social Engineering Defense  
- Acceptable Use & IT Policies (User Awareness)  
- User Support & Enablement  

---

## Devices
Endpoint security, enforcement, and technical hygiene.

### Endpoint Security
- Endpoint Protection (AV / EDR)  
- Disk Encryption  
- OS & Application Compliance  

### Backup & Recovery (Endpoint-Level)
- Endpoint Backups  
- Endpoint Restore Testing  

### Device Management & Enforcement
- Device Management Platform (MDM / RMM)  
- Patch Enforcement (Devices)  
- Local Admin Control  

### Inventory & Ownership
- Asset Inventory Accuracy  
- BYOD & Personal Devices  
- Device Lifecycle & Replacement
